Hypnotic Wheel

8 participants per facilitator
6 to 9 years old
30 to 45 minutes

This activity allows children to explore and better understand basic concepts of an electronic circuit.

They craft a cardboard wheel that they will connect to a battery motor to create a hypnotic effect while rotating. A fun way to explore the potential of visual arts created with electronics!
