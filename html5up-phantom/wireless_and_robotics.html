Wireless and Robotics

Project

This robotics and wireless technology workshop allows for participants to make a remotely controlled toy car, using littleBits modular circuits. Participants will learn together how to build an electronic circuit, and how to use parts such as wireless transmitters and receivers while having a good time playing with their toy car.

Age

8 to 12 years old

Number of participants

8 participants per mediator

Duration

2 hours

Languages

This workshop is available in both French and English.
